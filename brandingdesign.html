Sakura Ukulele

Peach&Fish Ukulele Studio is the influential Chinese online and offline teaching music studio. I was really excited to build their branding from the beginning, after we established the visual identity, alone with the design, we announce ukulele product, e.g. ukulele design, packaging, speaker, T-shirt, etc. Considering the major clients include children as well as young people, the main principle are : colourful, cute, warm and delightful. After we 4-years cooperation，Peach&Fish Ukulele Studio branding now is the top one Ukulele studio in China. We aim to spread the joy of music and we believe everyone can be a great musician.
