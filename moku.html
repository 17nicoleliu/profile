Project name: MOKU MUSIC Branding Design

Role -
Braning Design, Graphic Design, Illustration:

MOKU MUSIC is Chinese modern concept Ukulele teaching studio. We offer urbanities high-quality music experience and we keep the concept: music belong to everyone! Consist with the concept of MOKU MUSIC, I designed an minimalism, zen-tough, white outlook. White is associated with light, goodness, innocence, purity and virginity. It is considered to be the cooler of perfection.
